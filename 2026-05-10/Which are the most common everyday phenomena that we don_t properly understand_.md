### Main Topic: Science and AI

**Main Thesis:** Even advanced Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) may struggle to answer many complex scientific questions because the necessary experimental data is often missing.

**Simple Summary:** The author lists many complex scientific questions (like how lightning happens, why we dream, or how creatures develop) and asks whether Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) can answer them. The author suggests that AGI might fail at these tasks because the biggest challenge isn't reasoning, but the lack of real-world experimental data needed to form accurate answers.

**The Bottom Line:** > AI can reason, but it often lacks the necessary experimental data, meaning that many complex scientific questions cannot be fully answered by intelligence alone.
