### Main Topic: Oncology AI

**Main Thesis:** OncoAgent is an open-source system that uses advanced AI to provide safe, evidence-based treatment recommendations for cancer patients while keeping patient data private.

**Simple Summary:** This system is an advanced AI tool designed to help doctors make better decisions about cancer treatment. It solves the problem of outdated or conflicting medical guidelines by connecting AI to official cancer rules. It achieves this by using a structured system of specialized AI agents and a retrieval process that strictly follows medical guidelines, ensuring the advice is accurate and safe. Crucially, the system runs locally on hospital hardware, meaning patient data never leaves the hospital, ensuring total privacy.

**The Bottom Line:** > This technology allows hospitals to use powerful AI to provide highly accurate, personalized cancer advice that is safe, grounded in official guidelines, and completely private.
