### Main Topic: AI Capability Transfer

**Main Thesis:** The ability of an AI to use a learned skill depends heavily on the style or behavior it was trained to use, especially when the task is more complex.

**Simple Summary:** Researchers studied whether skills learned by AI models transfer when they change their style of answering (like using bold text or showing step-by-step reasoning). They found that for simple tasks, skills transfer easily regardless of the style. However, when the task requires deep thinking (like chess puzzles) or complex reasoning, the transfer of skills is much weaker, suggesting that the style of prompting is deeply connected to the actual knowledge the model possesses.

**The Bottom Line:** > The complexity of the task determines how much an AI's learned skills can be transferred between different ways of asking questions or presenting information.
