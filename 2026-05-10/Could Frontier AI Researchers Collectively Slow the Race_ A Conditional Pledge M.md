### Main Topic: AI Risk Control

**Main Thesis:** Researchers propose a system where they could collectively pause AI development to slow the progress and force urgent public and government action on safety.

**Simple Summary:** Many AI researchers are worried about the catastrophic risks of advanced AI, such as human extinction or mass control. They suggest that if enough researchers agree, they could voluntarily stop working on AI for a time. This action would serve to slow down the development race and bring immediate attention to the need for strong rules and safety measures for AI, similar to how other scientific fields manage major risks.

**The Bottom Line:** > To address extreme AI risks, researchers need a mechanism to collectively pause development, which can act as a powerful signal to governments to prioritize safety immediately.
