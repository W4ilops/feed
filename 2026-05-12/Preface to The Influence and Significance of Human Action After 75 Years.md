### Main Topic: Human Action

**Main Thesis:** Economics is not just about money or efficiency; it is about the fundamental rules that allow people to live together peacefully and prosperously.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that true economics is not just a tool for judging different policies or calculating wealth. Instead, economics reveals the basic truths about how human society is built and how people cooperate. The real importance of economic theory is not changing GDP, but ensuring the survival and well-being of humanity.

**The Bottom Line:** > Understanding economics is crucial because it guides us toward creating a stable society, not just maximizing financial numbers.
