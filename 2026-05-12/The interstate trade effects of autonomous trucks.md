### Main Topic: Trucking Trade

**Main Thesis:** Self-driving truck technology will significantly lower shipping costs, leading to a major increase in the value of goods traded across the United States.

**Simple Summary:** Studies show that using self-driving trucks will save money on shipping goods. This cost reduction is expected to boost the total amount of trade happening between states. The biggest financial benefits are seen in states like Texas and New York, and the industries that grow the most are vehicles, mixed freight, and electronics.

**The Bottom Line:** > Autonomous trucking technology has the potential to reshape how goods move across the country and create significant economic growth in key industries.
