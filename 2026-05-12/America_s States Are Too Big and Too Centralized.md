### Main Topic: US Structure

**Main Thesis:** The current way the US is organized causes conflict because states cannot act independently.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that the way the US is set up—with large, centralized states—is a broken system. States cannot leave or protect themselves from other states, and they cannot manage their own internal affairs. This setup only leads to growing conflict, meaning the only solution is to give more power to the central government in Washington.

**The Bottom Line:** > The current system is unstable and causes fighting; therefore, increasing centralized power is the only way to solve the problems.
