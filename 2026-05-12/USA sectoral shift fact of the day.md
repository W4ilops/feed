### Main Topic: Job Market Shift

**Main Thesis:** Healthcare and social assistance jobs have grown significantly while other industries have lost jobs recently.

**Simple Summary:** Since the end of 2023, the healthcare and social assistance sectors have created nearly 1.8 million new private-sector jobs in the U.S. Meanwhile, other industries have lost about 127,800 jobs.

**The Bottom Line:** > This shows that these specific sectors are growing jobs much faster than the rest of the economy, which raises questions about where the job growth is happening.
