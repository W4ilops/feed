### Main Topic: Reading education

**Main Thesis:** States that adopting phonics-based reading methods, combined with sustained teacher training and accountability, successfully improves student reading outcomes.

**Simple Summary:** To improve reading, states should use proven methods like phonics instead of older, less effective methods. States that successfully raised reading levels did so by making three key investments: training teachers in the new methods, providing clear accountability, and ensuring continuous support. This approach worked even when states did not spend a lot of extra money.

**The Bottom Line:** > Improving reading requires a sustained, multi-layered investment in training, curriculum, and accountability, which proves that smart strategies work regardless of budget.
