### Main Topic: AI Course Materials

**Main Thesis:** New materials for an intensive course on aligning AI systems are being released to allow others to learn the complex topics through independent study.

**Simple Summary:** This is a set of advanced course materials for a month-long course focused on how to make Artificial Intelligence behave safely and according to human goals. The course is designed for people who already have a strong background in math and computer science. The materials include exercises, lecture notes, and coding problems to teach the technical and philosophical challenges of aligning AI.

**The Bottom Line:** > Sharing these materials helps spread important knowledge about aligning AI and allows anyone interested to learn these complex concepts without needing to attend the full course.
