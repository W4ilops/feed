### Main Topic: Interest Rates

**Main Thesis:** How people's preferences for time affect the overall interest rate in the economy.

**Simple Summary:** The way people value their time determines the basic interest rate. While the market for loans sets one type of interest rate, the true rate is based on how people prefer to spend their money. If people prefer to save more (lower time preference), the interest rate tends to fall. If they prefer to spend more now (higher time preference), the rate tends to rise.

**The Bottom Line:** > Understanding individual time preferences is key to understanding why interest rates change during economic ups and downs.
