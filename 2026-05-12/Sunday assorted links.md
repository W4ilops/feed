### Main Topic: Trust and Discovery

**Main Thesis:** To make big discoveries, people should trust those who are exploring new ideas rather than those who only try to prove things wrong.

**Simple Summary:** When trying to learn new things, it is better to trust people who are in an "explorer mode"—people who are actively seeking new knowledge. It is tempting to trust people who are in "debunker mode," who only try to prove things are false. While debunker mode makes you feel right, it stops you from making big discoveries.

**The Bottom Line:** > Trust people who are exploring new ideas because that approach leads to the biggest breakthroughs.
