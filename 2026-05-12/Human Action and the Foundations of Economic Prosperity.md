### Main Topic: Scientific Disillusionment

**Main Thesis:** A character realizes that established economic theories and scientific equations are flawed and misused, leading to profound personal despair.

**Simple Summary:** A character named Hoxie confronts a colleague about the failure of established economic and scientific theories. He concludes that a famous thinker used flawed equations without understanding their true meaning, leading him to feel that all his previous work was worthless.

**The Bottom Line:** > The story shows that even great theories can be misleading, and relying on flawed systems can lead to deep disappointment and despair.
