### Main Topic: Resource Scarcity

**Main Thesis:** A project is only worthwhile if people value it more than other things they could be doing with the same resources.

**Simple Summary:** When people choose one project over another, they are choosing what they value most. If a project uses up resources like money and labor, it takes those resources away from other projects that people actually need more. This means that resources are limited, and we must recognize that there is not enough of everything.

**The Bottom Line:** > We must understand that resources are scarce, and ignoring this fact leads to poor decisions because we fail to see the true limits of what is possible.
