### Main Topic: Hugo Website Upgrade
**Main Thesis:** The author upgraded their old Hugo website (version 0.40 from 2018) to a newer version (0.135 from 2024), encountering numerous changes and requiring significant adjustments to templates and Markdown.
**Simple Summary:** This blog post documents the surprisingly complicated process of upgrading a long-standing website built with Hugo. The author had to change how the website’s header and navigation worked, update the way links to blog posts are handled, and completely revamp the way Markdown text is displayed. 
**The Bottom Line:** > It’s a reminder that even seemingly simple website updates can require a lot of work and careful attention to detail, and sometimes, upgrading software is more trouble than it’s worth.
