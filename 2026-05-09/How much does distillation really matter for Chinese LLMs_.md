**Simple Summary:** Some AI companies are teaching weaker AI models by copying the answers of stronger, more advanced AI models. This is a common way to train AI, but Chinese labs are suspected of doing it in a sneaky way to get better AI without building it themselves. They’re using fake accounts to trick powerful AI like Claude into giving them answers, and then using those answers to train their own models. This is causing concern because it could let other countries catch up faster than they should.
