### Main Topic: Open AI Models
**Main Thesis:** A long-term, stable approach to funding advanced open AI models requires a consortium of companies, not a single entity, to ensure consistent investment.
**Simple Summary:** The article says it’s becoming harder for companies to keep up with the newest AI models because it costs so much money. Instead of one company trying to do everything, it’s likely that many companies will release smaller, useful AI models that others can customize. Some companies like Nvidia are trying to lead the way, but others, especially Chinese startups, might struggle financially if they keep releasing models openly. Eventually, companies will need to release models that are good for smaller, specialized uses to stay competitive.
**The Bottom Line:** > The future of open AI models depends on a group of companies working together to fund them, and companies that don’t adapt to this new model could face financial challenges.
