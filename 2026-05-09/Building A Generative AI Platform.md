### Main Topic: Generative AI Platforms
**Main Thesis:** A generative AI platform needs a basic structure to take a question, get an answer from an AI model, and return it to the user, but can be expanded with tools for better results.
**Simple Summary:** This article explains how to build a system that uses AI to answer questions. It starts with a simple setup – asking a question and getting an answer – and then shows how you can add extra steps to make the answers better, like giving the AI more information to work with and adding safety measures.
**The Bottom Line:** > Adding extra steps to your AI system, like giving it more information and making sure it doesn’t give bad answers, is key to building a reliable and helpful tool.
