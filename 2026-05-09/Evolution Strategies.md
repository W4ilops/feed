### Main Topic: Black-Box Optimization
**Main Thesis:** The article explains how Evolution Strategies (ES) are a type of optimization method that can improve deep learning models even when we don’t know exactly how the model works.

**Simple Summary:**  Sometimes, it’s hard to figure out the best settings for a computer program (like a deep learning model). Evolution Strategies are like a smart way to try different settings over and over again, learning from what works best. It’s like nature’s way of survival – the best ideas get passed on. The article describes how these strategies work, including a more advanced version called CMA-ES that keeps track of how different settings relate to each other, making the process even smarter.

**The Bottom Line:** >  If you're stuck trying to improve a computer program, Evolution Strategies offer a powerful, flexible approach that can often find better solutions than traditional methods, especially when you don’t have all the information.
