### Main Topic: AI Risks and Priorities
**Main Thesis:** The article argues that the focus on catastrophic AI risks is misplaced, and that current AI alignment research is primarily driven by profit motives rather than genuine concern for existential threats.

**Simple Summary:** People are worried AI might become super-smart and cause problems, but the article suggests this worry is overblown. Instead, the main focus of AI development – by companies like OpenAI – is on making AI powerful and profitable, which is creating the conditions for these doomsday scenarios to be imagined. It’s like a cool toy that could become a problem.

**The Bottom Line:** > Don’t get too caught up in fears of AI destroying the world. The more important issue is how we’re developing AI now, and the potential for it to be used for profit, which could actually *cause* the problems we’re worried about.
