### Main Topic: Open Source AI Repositories
**Main Thesis:** The article analyzes the evolving landscape of open-source AI repositories, specifically focusing on foundation models and outlining a layered “AI stack” to understand the current development trends.
**Simple Summary:** This article catalogs and categorizes over 800 open-source AI projects, primarily around tools and software used to build and use AI models. It breaks down how these projects fit into three layers: the basic tools needed to run AI, the tools for actually building AI models, and the tools for using those models to create applications. The article also explains why the excitement around AI has cooled down a bit recently.
**The Bottom Line:** > Understanding this “AI stack” helps you see how the open-source AI community is organized and where the most active development is happening – it’s a rapidly changing field, but these layers provide a useful framework for keeping up with the latest advancements.
