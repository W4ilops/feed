### Main Topic: Multimodal AI
**Main Thesis:** The article explains how AI is moving beyond just understanding one type of data (like text or images) to being able to use multiple types of data together, leading to more powerful and versatile AI systems.

**Simple Summary:**  AI used to only understand one thing at a time – like just reading text or just looking at pictures. But now, scientists are building AI that can understand *everything* – like reading text, seeing images, and hearing sounds all at once. This is called “multimodal AI,” and it’s making AI much smarter and able to do more complex things, like helping blind people navigate the world.

**The Bottom Line:** > AI is getting better at understanding the world by combining different types of information, which will lead to AI that can solve more problems and do more helpful things in the future.
