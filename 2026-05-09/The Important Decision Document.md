### Main Topic: Project Decisions
**Main Thesis:** A document called an “Important Decisions Doc” helps teams avoid wasting time arguing about past decisions and keeps everyone on the same page.
**Simple Summary:** This article describes how a team at Stack Overflow created a simple record of important decisions made during a long project. When someone asks why a decision was made, it can cause arguments and delays. The “Important Decisions Doc” solves this by listing all the decisions, explaining why they were made, and letting everyone revisit them calmly to ensure everyone understands and agrees. It’s like a reference guide that helps new team members, prevents arguments, and keeps decision-makers accountable.
**The Bottom Line:** > By having a clear record of past decisions and revisiting them thoughtfully, teams can avoid unnecessary conflict, improve morale, and move forward more efficiently.
