### Main Topic: AI and Software Development
**Main Thesis:** The rise of AI in software development is changing what it means to be a developer, leading to a sense of diminished purpose and a widening gap between desired work and actual work.

**Simple Summary:** This article explores how AI is changing the job of a software developer. It’s not just about writing code; developers are spending less time on the parts of their jobs they actually enjoy and more time on things like meetings and fixing problems.  AI tools were supposed to make things easier, but they’re actually making developers feel less fulfilled and less productive in the work they find meaningful.

**The Bottom Line:** > Developers need to rethink how they use AI, focusing on tasks that truly require human creativity and problem-solving, rather than simply automating repetitive tasks, to maintain job satisfaction and a sense of purpose.
