### Main Topic: AI Alignment
**Main Thesis:** Rational AI shouldn’t be given goals because humans don’t have goals, and AI should instead be designed to align with how humans naturally act and make decisions.
**Simple Summary:** This article argues that instead of trying to tell AI what to *want*, we should teach it to act in ways that match how humans naturally behave. It suggests that AI should be built to follow patterns of action and values, like a musician following a melody, rather than being given specific goals. This approach, called “eudaimonic rationality,” could make AI safer and more reliable.
**The Bottom Line:** > It’s better to design AI to learn and act like humans, rather than trying to force them to pursue specific goals, because this creates a more stable and trustworthy system.
