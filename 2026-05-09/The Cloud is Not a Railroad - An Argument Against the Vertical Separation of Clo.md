### Main Topic: Cloud Regulation
**Main Thesis:** The proposed regulation of cloud providers by separating their services is a flawed idea that would ultimately harm innovation and software quality.
**Simple Summary:** This article argues against splitting up cloud companies into separate parts. It says that cloud providers offer many different services, and limiting those services would actually make it harder to build good software. The author believes cloud technology has evolved to be more efficient, and that forcing a separation would stop that progress.
**The Bottom Line:** > Regulating cloud providers in this way is a bad idea because it would stifle innovation and limit the ability to build high-quality software, ultimately hindering the growth and evolution of cloud technology.
