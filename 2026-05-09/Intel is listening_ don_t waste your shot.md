### Main Topic: Customer Feedback
**Main Thesis:** The author argues that customers can significantly impact a hardware vendor’s product development by providing direct, honest, and well-prepared feedback, but this requires strategic communication and assertive behavior.
**Simple Summary:** This article shares the experiences of a former Intel employee who realized they weren’t effectively using their power as a customer to influence Intel’s product development. The author offers practical advice on how to give constructive criticism during meetings, including preparing beforehand, protecting intellectual property, and being firm about your concerns – even if it means challenging the team. 

**The Bottom Line:** > Don’t be afraid to speak up and provide honest feedback to hardware vendors, but do so strategically and with a clear plan to ensure your voice is heard and valued.
