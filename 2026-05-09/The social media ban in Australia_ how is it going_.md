### Main Topic: Social Media Ban
**Main Thesis:** Australia’s ban on young social media users hasn’t worked because teens still think their friends are using the platforms and don’t feel like they’re popular if they don’t use them.
**Simple Summary:** Australia recently banned kids under 16 from using big social media sites, but it’s not really working. A study found that most teens still use these platforms, even though they’re banned, because they think their friends are still using them and they worry about being seen as unpopular if they don’t.
**The Bottom Line:** > This ban is failing because it doesn’t address the social pressures driving teens to use these platforms. To be effective, future bans need to change how teens see themselves and their friends’ behavior around social media.
