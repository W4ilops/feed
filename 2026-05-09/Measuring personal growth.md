### Main Topic: Personal Growth Metrics
**Main Thesis:** The article explores alternative ways to measure personal growth beyond traditional metrics like wealth or social media followers, suggesting three key indicators: rate of change, time to solve problems, and the number of future options.
**Simple Summary:** The author realized that founders often obsess over business growth, so they wondered if we can measure personal growth too. They came up with three ways to track it: noticing big shifts in your life every few years, tackling major life challenges (like career, family, and finances), and having a variety of potential future goals and dreams.
**The Bottom Line:** > It’s okay to measure your growth to understand yourself better and make intentional choices about the direction you want your life to take – but don’t let the measurement overshadow the joy of living.
