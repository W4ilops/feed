### Main Topic: Performance Teams
**Main Thesis:** Companies should form performance engineering teams to significantly reduce costs, improve reliability, and gain a competitive edge through infrastructure optimization.
**Simple Summary:** Businesses are spending too much money on computers and software. Performance engineers are experts who can find ways to make things run faster and cheaper, saving companies money and improving how well their services work. They work with developers and other teams to find hidden ways to cut costs and make sure everything runs smoothly, especially when lots of people are using a service at once.
**The Bottom Line:** > Investing in a performance team can lead to substantial cost savings and improved service reliability, ultimately boosting a company's bottom line and competitive position.
