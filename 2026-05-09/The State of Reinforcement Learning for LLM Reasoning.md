### Main Topic: LLM Reasoning
**Main Thesis:** The excitement around new large language models is fading because they aren’t specifically trained to think like humans, while competitors are adding reasoning abilities.
**Simple Summary:** Big language models like GPT-4 are getting better, but they aren’t really *thinking* in a helpful way. Companies like xAI and Anthropic are adding features that let the models explain their thinking process. OpenAI is now focusing on training models to think more strategically, using a special method called reinforcement learning. This means the models learn by getting rewards for correct answers and penalties for wrong ones, making them better at solving tricky problems.
**The Bottom Line:** >  Simply making models bigger doesn't guarantee they'll be good at problem-solving; specifically training them to think step-by-step is the key to improving their intelligence.
