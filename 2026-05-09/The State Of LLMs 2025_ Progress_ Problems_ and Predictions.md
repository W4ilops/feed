### Main Topic: Reasoning Language Models
**Main Thesis:** The article argues that 2025 saw a significant shift in LLM development, primarily driven by new methods for enabling reasoning abilities within models.
**Simple Summary:** In 2025, AI language models got better at “thinking” thanks to a new technique called RLVR. This technique allows models to explain their answers and solve problems more effectively, like a human would. A key moment was when a new, open AI model called DeepSeek R1 showed this was possible, leading other companies to follow suit. It’s now cheaper to train these models than people thought, and they’re becoming a standard feature in many AI systems.
**The Bottom Line:** > AI language models are now capable of more complex problem-solving, and training them is becoming more affordable, meaning we’ll likely see even smarter AI systems in the future.
