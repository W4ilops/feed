### Main Topic: Better Man Pages
**Main Thesis:** The author is exploring ways to make man pages easier to use by incorporating elements of cheat sheets, like summaries and categories, and improving navigation.
**Simple Summary:** This article discusses how frustrating it can be to find the right information in a man page. The author wants to redesign man pages to be more like cheat sheets, with quick summaries and organized sections, making it easier for people to quickly find what they need.
