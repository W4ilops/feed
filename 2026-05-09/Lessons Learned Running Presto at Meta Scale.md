### Main Topic: Scaling Query Engines
**Main Thesis:** The article describes how Meta scaled its use of the Presto query engine to handle a massive number of users and data, detailing the strategies and automation they used.
**Simple Summary:** Meta uses a tool called Presto to quickly ask questions about their huge amounts of data. As they got more and more people using Presto, they had to figure out how to update the tool and add more computers to handle the load. They solved this by automatically updating the software, adding new computers when needed, and making sure everyone could still use Presto while things were changing.
**The Bottom Line:** > Keeping Presto running smoothly at a large company requires smart automation and careful planning to ensure consistent performance and availability for all users.
