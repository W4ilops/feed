### Main Topic: Neural Network Design
**Main Thesis:** The article explains how computers can automatically design better neural networks, instead of relying solely on human experts.
**Simple Summary:** Scientists are trying to teach computers to build their own smart networks. They do this by letting the computer try out different designs and seeing which ones work best. The computer looks for the best way to connect different parts of the network, like layers, to make it really good at a task. It’s like a game where the computer tries to win by building the most effective network.
**The Bottom Line:** > Automatically designing networks could lead to much faster and more efficient AI systems because computers can explore more possibilities than humans ever could.
