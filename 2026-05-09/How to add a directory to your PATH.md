### Main Topic: Adding Programs to Your Computer

**Main Thesis:** This article explains how to add folders where computer programs live to a list of places your computer looks for them, so you can run those programs easily.

**Simple Summary:** The article walks you through adding folders with programs to your computer’s “path.” It shows you how to figure out which computer program you’re trying to use, where it’s located, and then how to tell your computer to look in that folder when you type the program’s name. It covers different types of computers (like Macs and Linux) and how to find the right folder.

**The Bottom Line:** > If you can’t run a program you installed, figuring out where it’s located and adding that folder to your computer’s list of places to look is a simple fix that will get it working.
