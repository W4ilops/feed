### Main Topic: Artificial Intelligence Understanding
**Main Thesis:** The current excitement about AI is based on a flawed idea – that simply making AI bigger will automatically make it truly intelligent.

**Simple Summary:** This article argues that today’s AI, like large language models, doesn’t really *understand* the world. It’s just really good at predicting what words should come next. Because it’s built to predict, it creates a fake understanding of how things work, which makes it seem smarter than it actually is. True intelligence needs to be connected to the real world and how we interact with it.

**The Bottom Line:** > Don’t be fooled by impressive AI tricks; real intelligence needs a basic understanding of how things work in the physical world, something current AI models lack.
