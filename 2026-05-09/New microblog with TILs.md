### Main Topic: Personal Knowledge Base
**Main Thesis:** The author created a simple section on their website called “TIL” (Today I Learned) to quickly store and easily find useful tips and tools they discover.
**Simple Summary:** The author made a new area on their website for quick notes and reminders about cool things they find online. It’s like a personal collection of helpful links and tools, styled to look like tweets. They created it mostly for themselves because they often forget things, and it’s inspired by a similar blog they admire. They want to keep this area separate from their main blog and social media, focusing on things they want to keep and share long-term.
**The Bottom Line:** > Having a dedicated space for quick notes and reminders can save you time and prevent you from forgetting valuable information – it’s a simple way to build a personal knowledge base.
