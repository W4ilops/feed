### Main Topic: AI Application Pitfalls
**Main Thesis:** The article argues that many teams misuse foundation models and fail to consider user experience, leading to unsuccessful AI applications.
**Simple Summary:** This article warns about common mistakes when using powerful AI tools. It highlights that simply using AI isn’t enough; teams often try to apply AI to problems where it’s not needed, or they focus on the technology itself instead of the user’s actual needs. For example, some teams tried to use AI to automatically manage energy bills, but a simpler approach was more effective. Similarly, teams dismissed AI as a solution when the real problem was a poor user experience – users didn’t want summaries of meetings, they wanted action items, and they didn’t want blunt assessments of their skills.
**The Bottom Line:** > Don’t automatically assume AI is the answer; focus on solving the user’s real problem first, and always prioritize a good user experience when building AI-powered products.
