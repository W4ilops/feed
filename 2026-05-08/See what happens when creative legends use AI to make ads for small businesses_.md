### Main Topic: AI & Small Business
**Main Thesis:** Three experienced advertising experts are using AI tools to create high-quality marketing campaigns for local businesses.
**Simple Summary:** Google is helping three famous ad people – Jayanta Jenkins, Tiffany Rolfe, and Susan Credle – make amazing ads for small businesses. They’re using a special AI program called Flow to create professional-looking campaigns that will help these businesses attract more customers. The final ads will be shown in June.
**The Bottom Line:** > Small businesses can now get top-notch marketing help thanks to AI, allowing them to compete with bigger companies and reach more people. This shows how AI can be a powerful tool for growth.
