### Main Topic: California Wildfires
**Main Thesis:** The state of California’s policies have worsened wildfires, making them more destructive.
**Simple Summary:** The article says California’s government controls almost all of the land where wildfires happen. Because they haven’t taken good care of this land and have changed how insurance works, more people live in dangerous areas, and the fires become much bigger and more deadly.
**The Bottom Line:** > California’s government decisions are directly contributing to the severity of its wildfires, and this needs to change to protect people and property.
