### Main Topic: California Housing
**Main Thesis:** The article argues that democratic socialist ideas have significantly contributed to California’s housing shortage by hindering development.
**Simple Summary:** The article says that in the 1960s, certain political ideas – like wanting everyone to have a say in city planning, focusing on protecting the environment, and stopping economic growth – gave people and government officials ways to block new homes being built. This has led to a lack of affordable housing in California.
**The Bottom Line:** > Democratic socialist ideas, when applied to urban planning and environmental regulations, have unintentionally created a major housing crisis in California by preventing new construction.
