### Main Topic: AI Models & Experts
**Main Thesis:** Researchers have created a new type of AI model that can be easily divided into smaller, specialized parts, allowing it to be used more efficiently.
**Simple Summary:**  AI models are getting huge and complicated, but they don't always need *all* their power for every job. This new AI, called EMO, is designed to work like a team of specialists. It can use only a small group of its experts for a task, while still performing well overall.  It’s smarter about how it divides up its work, so it’s easier to use and doesn’t waste resources.
**The Bottom Line:** > This new AI model makes large AI systems more practical and efficient by allowing them to be broken down into smaller, specialized parts, improving how they use computing power.
