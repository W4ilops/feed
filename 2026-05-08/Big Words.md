### Main Topic: Web Slide Maker
**Main Thesis:** A tool has been created to easily generate text slides for presentations by accepting URL parameters.
**Simple Summary:** Someone made a simple website that lets you quickly create slides with text. It’s a quick way to make a slide without needing complicated software.
