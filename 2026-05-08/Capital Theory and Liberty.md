### Main Topic: Freedom vs. Socialism
**Main Thesis:** The article argues that economics needs to seriously consider how the idea of “capital” – and its connection to freedom versus unfreedom – is central to understanding the failures of socialist movements.
**Simple Summary:** This article says that the idea of “capital” (basically, things like factories and machines) has a long history tied to the fight for freedom. Socialism argued that capital was used to control people, and many socialist attempts to get rid of private capital led to terrible disasters. But mainstream economics ignores this history, treating capital as just another tool like labor, without considering its impact on freedom.
**The Bottom Line:** > Economists need to revisit the historical link between capital and freedom to avoid repeating the mistakes of past socialist experiments and truly understand the importance of economic liberty.
