### Main Topic: AI Transparency
**Main Thesis:** The European Union is creating rules to make sure people know when they're talking to or seeing AI-generated content, like deepfakes.
**Simple Summary:** The EU wants to make it easier for people to tell if they’re talking to a computer or seeing fake videos or articles. They’re asking companies and people to share their ideas on how to do this, and soon, AI systems will need to tell you when they’re being used and mark fake content as such. This is to protect people from being tricked by AI.
**The Bottom Line:** > Companies and individuals need to tell people when they’re interacting with AI and clearly label fake content to avoid misleading the public.
