### Main Topic: AI Command Generation
**Main Thesis:** The article explores how to make small AI models better at generating safe and correct commands for computers, specifically using a technique called constrained decoding.

**Simple Summary:** AI is getting smarter at using computers, but it’s often unreliable when it comes to typing commands. This article describes a new way to teach small AI models to write better commands – like Bash commands – by creating rules that limit what the AI can type. It uses a tool called “grammargen” to automatically create these rules from examples of how commands are used. The goal is to make AI agents more dependable and secure.

**The Bottom Line:** > It’s crucial to ensure AI can reliably and safely control computers because mistakes in commands could have serious consequences, and this new method offers a way to improve that reliability.
