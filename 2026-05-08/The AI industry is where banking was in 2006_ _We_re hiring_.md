### Main Topic: AI Safety
**Main Thesis:** The article argues that, like with financial risks 20 years ago, the world is failing to properly manage the risks posed by rapidly advancing artificial intelligence.
**Simple Summary:** A group called CeSIA (a French AI safety center) is looking for people to help fix this problem. They’re worried that AI is growing too fast and that governments aren’t doing enough to keep it safe. They want to make sure rules about AI are strict and that everyone understands the potential dangers. They’re hiring a policy expert and a communications person to help them push for these changes.
**The Bottom Line:** > We need to act now to ensure AI development doesn’t become uncontrolled, and CeSIA is trying to lead the way in making sure governments and companies take AI safety seriously.
