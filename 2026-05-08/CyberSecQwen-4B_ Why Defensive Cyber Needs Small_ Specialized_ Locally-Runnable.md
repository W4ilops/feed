### Main Topic: AI for Cybersecurity
**Main Thesis:** The article argues that using large AI models like ChatGPT for cybersecurity defense is too expensive, risky, and impractical for organizations needing to protect sensitive data and operate in secure environments.

**Simple Summary:** Cybersecurity teams need to protect critical information and operate securely, but using powerful AI tools like ChatGPT isn't a good solution. These tools are costly, require sending data to outside servers, and can't handle the sensitive information needed for investigations. A smaller, locally-run AI model is a better choice for specific tasks.

**The Bottom Line:** > Don’t rely on expensive, cloud-based AI for critical cybersecurity tasks. A smaller, locally-controlled AI model can be more effective, secure, and cost-efficient for handling sensitive data and investigations.
