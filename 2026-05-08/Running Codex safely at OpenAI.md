### Main Topic: AI Agent Control
**Main Thesis:** The article explains how OpenAI is building AI agents that can automatically do coding tasks, but also how they’re making sure these agents don’t cause problems.
**Simple Summary:** OpenAI created AI agents that can help programmers with their work, like reviewing code and running commands. However, they’ve put in lots of rules and controls to keep these agents safe and prevent them from doing anything harmful. They limit what the agents can access, require approval for risky actions, and track everything they do so people can understand what’s happening. 

**The Bottom Line:** > AI agents are powerful, but it’s crucial to have strong controls in place to ensure they’re used safely and responsibly, and to understand their actions.
