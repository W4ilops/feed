### Main Topic: UC System Decline
**Main Thesis:** The University of California has become a prime example of how government money can lead universities to prioritize ideas over good results and to avoid being held responsible for how they spend money.

**Simple Summary:** This article says that the University of California used to be a really great research school, but it’s now struggling because it gets almost all its money from the state. Because of this, the university doesn't have to worry about making smart choices with its money or changing its ideas if they aren't working. Instead, it focuses on sticking with popular ideas, even if they aren’t the best.

**The Bottom Line:** > The way universities get money from the government can change them in ways that aren't good for students or for learning, and it’s important to find better ways to manage university funding.
