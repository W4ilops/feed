### Main Topic: AI Deployment Issues

**Main Thesis:** Moving an AI model from training to live use is often full of hidden problems that waste time and money, and there are specific steps to fix them.

**Simple Summary:** When building an AI model, the process of getting it ready for real use (deployment) is often messy and full of hidden errors, which slows down the project and increases costs. These problems, called "pipeline friction," happen when converting the model from its training format into a format that computers can run efficiently. The article provides practical steps—like checking exports early and simplifying the model structure—to eliminate these common problems.

**The Bottom Line:** > By following specific rules for exporting and optimizing AI models, teams can avoid costly errors and ensure their AI systems run faster and more reliably in the real world.
