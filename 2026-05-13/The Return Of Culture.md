### Main Topic: Power Influence

**Main Thesis:** The relative influence of capital, culture, and states has significantly changed throughout history.

**Simple Summary:** An analysis of historical periods shows that the power of capital, culture, and states has shifted over time. Before the industrial age, states and capital were less influential. Later, states became the most powerful force, but recently, culture has taken the lead, making states and capital much weaker.

**The Bottom Line:** > Understanding how these three forces influence each other changes depending on the historical period you are looking at.
