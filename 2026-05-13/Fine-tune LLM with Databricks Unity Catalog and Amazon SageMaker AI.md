### Main Topic: LLM Fine-tuning

**Main Thesis:** You can securely fine-tune large language models while maintaining strict data governance and tracking all steps using a combination of Databricks and AWS services.

**Simple Summary:** When you train an AI model (LLM), you need to make sure the data used is secure and properly tracked. This article shows how to safely combine Databricks' data management system (Unity Catalog) with AWS's machine learning tools (SageMaker) to fine-tune an AI model. The solution ensures that the data used for training is governed by strict rules, and the entire process—from preparing the data to training the model—is tracked, keeping security and compliance intact.

**The Bottom Line:** > By linking these systems, you can perform complex AI training securely, ensuring that all data lineage and access permissions are strictly controlled and auditable.
