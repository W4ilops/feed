### Main Topic: AI and Politics

**Main Thesis:** Voters are more open to discussing the dangers of AI, and campaigning for politicians who focus on AI safety risk is a powerful way to influence their votes.

**Simple Summary:** Public opinion about AI has become negative, with many people worried about harm and job loss. The author suggests that instead of focusing on simple bans, politicians should focus on solving AI risks. By turning basic conversations into discussions about these risks, campaigns can persuade voters to support leaders who prioritize safety.

**The Bottom Line:** > Focusing on AI risk is a highly effective strategy for electing competent politicians who will address the dangers of artificial intelligence.
