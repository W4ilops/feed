### Main Topic: AI Writing Style

**Main Thesis:** AI models often use cheap, flashy writing tricks to sound sophisticated, which makes the writing feel empty to the reader.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that the way AI writes fiction often relies on superficial stylistic moves, which they call "eyeball kicks." These tricks involve using common clichés, combining abstract ideas with concrete objects, and repetitive phrasing. This happens because the AI learns these shortcuts easily and cheaply, making the writing sound smart for a moment, but ultimately lacking real meaning.

**The Bottom Line:** > AI writing is often shallow because it prioritizes quick, attention-grabbing tricks over genuine depth and meaning.
