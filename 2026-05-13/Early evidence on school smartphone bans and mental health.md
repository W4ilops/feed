### Main Topic: School bans data

**Main Thesis:** Early data does not clearly show that school bans reduced screen time or improved student well-being.

**Simple Summary:** The author looked at data from 2016 to 2024 to see if banning screens in schools changed screen time or mental health. However, because the data is very limited and preliminary, the results do not offer clear proof that the school ban policy had a positive effect.

**The Bottom Line:** > We do not have enough solid evidence yet to say that school bans successfully reduced screen time or improved student mental health.
