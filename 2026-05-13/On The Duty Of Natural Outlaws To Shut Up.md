### Main Topic: Radical Movement

**Main Thesis:** People do not join radical movements simply for small material gains, but for much deeper, more serious reasons.

**Simple Summary:** The article asks why people would commit themselves to difficult, marginalized lives and fighting for a radical cause just for minor things, like a little more soap or candy. It suggests that serious movements are driven by much larger, more important goals than simple consumer desires.

**The Bottom Line:** > True radical action is motivated by profound beliefs, not by small, trivial wants.
