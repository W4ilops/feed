### Main Topic: Coaching results

**Main Thesis:** Most testimonials for personal training and inner work focus on feelings and experiences rather than showing clients tangible, lasting improvements in their lives.

**Simple Summary:** The author argues that while many coaches and programs focus on intense experiences and good feelings, they often fail to show clients concrete, long-term results, such as stopping bad habits or achieving major life goals. The article suggests that if the goal of inner work is to improve life, practitioners should highlight specific, measurable changes that clients actually started doing or achieved, instead of just focusing on the experience itself.

**The Bottom Line:** > When looking for personal coaching or inner work, focus on testimonials that describe specific, lasting changes in what a person does, avoids, or achieves, not just intense feelings.
