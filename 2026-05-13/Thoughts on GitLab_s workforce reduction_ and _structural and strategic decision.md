### Main Topic: Company Restructuring

**Main Thesis:** Companies like GitLab are restructuring their organizations to create smaller, more independent teams, driven by the massive demand created by the rise of agentic software.

**Simple Summary:** GitLab is making big changes to its company structure, flattening management layers and creating many smaller, independent teams to empower engineers. This change is tied to the "agentic era," where the cost of making software is dropping rapidly. The author believes that because software is becoming cheaper to create, there will be much higher demand for software and the people who build it.

**The Bottom Line:** > The collapse of software costs means there will be a huge increase in demand for software and developers, which is a powerful force for business growth.
