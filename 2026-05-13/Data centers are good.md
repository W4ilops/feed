### Main Topic: Data Center Impact

**Main Thesis:** Building data centers creates measurable positive economic activity and changes local prices in the areas where they are located.

**Simple Summary:** This study looked at how building data centers affects local communities in the U.S. It found that data centers bring jobs (construction, processing, and general employment), increase property values, and raise local electricity prices. Essentially, data centers create a local economic boost, but they also put pressure on local resources like land and power.

**The Bottom Line:** > Data centers are not just technology; they are physical developments that significantly impact local economies, job markets, and utility costs.
