### Main Topic: Cloud traffic rules

**Main Thesis:** Cloudflare can now set traffic rules based on where the traffic originates, not just where it is going.

**Simple Summary:** The Cloudflare One Appliance can now use the source of the traffic (like a specific network or IP address) to decide how to handle that traffic. This means you can easily separate guest networks or prioritize traffic from a specific group without having to look at every single destination application.

**The Bottom Line:** > This makes managing network traffic much simpler and more flexible, allowing you to quickly control and prioritize data based on its origin.
