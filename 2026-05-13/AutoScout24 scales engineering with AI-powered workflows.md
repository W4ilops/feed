### Main Topic: AI in Business

**Main Thesis:** By strategically integrating AI tools into daily workflows, companies can significantly speed up development, improve code quality, and accelerate innovation.

**Simple Summary:** A large car marketplace used a two-part plan to adopt AI: giving employees general access to tools like ChatGPT and embedding a specific coding assistant (Codex) directly into the engineering work. This approach helped employees use AI for practical tasks, like reviewing code and writing documentation. By focusing on real-world results and creating a network of internal experts, the company was able to cut development time from weeks down to days and improve the quality of their software.

**The Bottom Line:** > Using AI tools integrated into daily work is a powerful way to make software development faster and higher quality, leading to quicker results for customers and partners.
