### Main Topic: AI Credit Reporting

**Main Thesis:** Users can download usage reports to estimate their AI credit costs and plan their budgets.

**Simple Summary:** GitHub Copilot users can download a report to see how much AI credit they have used. This report helps people figure out who is using the most AI, which models are used the most, and estimate how much money they will spend each month.

**The Bottom Line:** > This report is for planning costs and spotting patterns, not for calculating the final bill; treat the numbers as an estimate to help you budget.
