### Main Topic: Document Schema Automation

**Main Thesis:** A new feature automatically analyzes large collections of unknown documents to create the necessary structure (schema) needed for smart information extraction.

**Simple Summary:** Creating a structure for documents is a difficult manual task, especially when dealing with thousands of unknown files. This solution fixes that by automatically looking at a pile of documents, grouping similar ones together, and instantly creating the necessary structure definitions. This process prepares the documents so that other systems can easily read and extract information from them.

**The Bottom Line:** > This automation saves massive amounts of manual work, allowing you to quickly prepare large document collections for advanced information processing.
