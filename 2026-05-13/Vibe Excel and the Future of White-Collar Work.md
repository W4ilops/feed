### Main Topic: AI Adoption

**Main Thesis:** The biggest barrier to using AI in most jobs is not the AI's intelligence, but the organization's reluctance to trust and integrate it.

**Simple Summary:** While AI tools are getting better, the main challenge for companies adopting AI is not the technology itself, but the resistance to change. This resistance, called "adoption inertia," means that how quickly AI spreads across different jobs depends more on organizational willingness to trust and change processes than on how smart the AI models are.

**The Bottom Line:** > Companies should focus on building trust and creating easy ways to integrate AI into daily work, because organizational resistance will be the main bottleneck for future AI growth.
